# Foundations

The design language: tokens and what they mean. Each concept's `tokens` frontmatter is canonical;
[`/styles/tokens.css`](/styles/tokens.css) is the runnable projection (light + dark).

* [Color](color.md) - Functional fg/bg/border roles that re-theme light↔dark.
* [Typography](typography.md) - Mona Sans over a system stack; 14px base.
* [Spacing](spacing.md) - The 4px-based base-size scale.
* [Elevation](elevation.md) - Layered, low-alpha shadows.
* [Shape](shape.md) - Restrained corner-radius scale.
* [Motion](motion.md) - Quick transitions; reduced-motion first.
* [Layout & responsive](layout.md) - 12-column grid, four breakpoints.
