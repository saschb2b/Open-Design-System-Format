# Foundations

The design language: tokens and what they mean. Each concept's frontmatter is canonical;
[`/styles/tokens.css`](/styles/tokens.css) is the runnable projection (light + dark).

* [Color](color.md) - The numbered, theme-able scales (gray + 7 accents, 100→1000).
* [Typography](typography.md) - Geist Sans / Geist Mono and the type scale.
* [Grid](grid.md) - The 4px geometric grid and breakpoints.
* [Shape](shape.md) - The 6px-anchored corner-radius scale.
* [Elevation](elevation.md) - 1px hairlines first; soft shadows for floating layers.
* [Materials](materials.md) - Layered surface and translucent-gray materials.
* [Icons](icons.md) - Geist Icons, a developer-tools line-icon set.
