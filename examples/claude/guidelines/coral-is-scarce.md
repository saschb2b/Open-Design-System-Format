# Rule
`{colors.primary}` (coral) appears on **one** primary CTA per view and on the **single** full-bleed
[coral callout](/components/callout-card-coral.md) per page. Everywhere else, surfaces are cream or
dark.

# Why
The coral is the brand's voltage. Voltage works because it is rare — one coral button in a cream
field draws the eye exactly where intended. Paint borders, icons, badges, and links coral too, and
nothing leads; the page reads cheap and the signature accent loses its charge.

# Do
Keep one `.btn--primary` per view and one coral band per page; let the featured pricing tier signal
itself with the dark surface, not a coral border.

# Don't
Spray coral across buttons, borders, icons, and headings — see
[coral-is-scarce.dont.html](/guidelines/coral-is-scarce.dont.html).
