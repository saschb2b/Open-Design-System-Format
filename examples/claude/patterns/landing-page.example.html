Claude by Anthropic

Meet your thinking partner

Claude helps you write, analyze, and build — thinking through hard problems step by step, with a calm, considered voice.

quickstart.py
import anthropic

client = anthropic.Anthropic()
message = client.messages.create(
    model="claude-opus-4-8",
    max_tokens=1024,
    messages=[{"role": "user", "content": "Hello, Claude"}],
)
print(message.content)
